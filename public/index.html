How to Play

Basic Rules

  • Take turns placing and launching pucks
  • Click/tap once to place your puck
  • Click/tap again anywhere to launch it
  • Each player gets 5 pucks per game
  • Wait for opponent's pucks to stop before your turn

Game Modes

Collision Mode

Score points by hitting opponent's pucks with yours. Faster hits score more points!

Territory Mode

Control territory by placing pucks strategically. Score from both territory control and collisions.
